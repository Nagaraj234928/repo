About

SRKMHSS was Established in 1978 with modest infrastructure and a handful of 15 students. However, there were 3 outstanding features which distinguished this school from all other institutions in the Vellore district. Active involvement of parents, school, and community – the three catalysts of change. Incorporation of the philosophy of holistic education (which can be explained in terms of good teachers, overall development of children, rich infrastructure and other elements). A notable emphasis on the moral education of children. These were sure to pay dividends! Today SRKMHSS has the strength of nearly 3,000 students and a teaching fraternity of 130 well-trained & qualified teachers.

Our Vision

Sri Ramakrishna Matric. Hr. Sec. School will create a safe and structured environment where students and staff, along with parents and guardians, come together as a community of life-long learners. Our school will foster individual differences, encourage each other, and set challenging goals. We will strive to think critically and creatively to encourage good decision-making strategies to promote a sense of teamwork and cooperation. By learning how to utilize the knowledge and tools necessary to confidently meet challenges, our students will be empowered to reach their potential, as well as become respectful and responsible citizens for the future.

Our Mission

The mission of Sri Ramakrishna Matric. Hr. Sec. School is to promote academic excellence and provide the opportunity for early adolescents to attain their individual, social, emotional, and physical potential. Quality education will be provided for all students through teamwork and cooperation resulting in their development into mature productive citizens.

Dedicated to Excellence Bhagavan shree Ramakrishna Paramahamsa Matriculation Hr. Sec. School is affiliated with Matriculation Board of Tamil Nadu State. It has been situated in the heart of Hosur Municipality, since 1986. The campus is filled with lush green, calm and creates a pleasant atmosphere for teaching and learning process. It is a co-educational institution and following "Samacheer" syllabus with teaching and learning and assessing process. It has classes from LKG to XII Std. This curriculum enables the children to foster a continuous and comprehensive education and liberal to all children from all communities. This is the school run by Arul House of Charitable Trust aims to create a "Whole Child". It is giving importance to Scholastic and Co-scholastic areas of development. The teaching and learning process in the school is to efficiently and effectively address the wide range developmental characteristics of the learner. The school facilitates the learning process through different learning styles and integrated curriculum. The learning is non-demanding, non-threatening environment. The students are accompanied through Continuous and Comprehensive Evaluation, to help them to achieve the desired competencies. Child-centered and activity based teaching and learning process is an important dimension of the school.
